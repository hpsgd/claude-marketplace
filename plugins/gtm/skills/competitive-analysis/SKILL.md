Analyse $ARGUMENTS competitively.

## For each competitor

1. **What they do** — one paragraph
2. **Target customer** — who they serve
3. **Pricing model** — how they charge
4. **Strengths** — what they do well (be honest)
5. **Weaknesses** — where they fall short
6. **Positioning** — how they describe themselves
7. **Differentiation** — how we differ

## Output

Present as a comparison table, then a written analysis of where the biggest differentiation opportunities are. Be specific — "better UX" is not differentiation. "3-step onboarding vs their 12-step wizard" is.
