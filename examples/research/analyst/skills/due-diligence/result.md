- Revenue growth decelerated from 32% to 10.8% over two years — now below market CAGR
- No new equity funding since July 2021 (nearly 4 years)
- Cash runway estimated at ~18 months from FY25 year-end (late 2026)
- Blackbird marked down Culture Amp by 23.5% (November 2025) — first public acknowledgment of valuation decline. Source: [Startup Daily, Nov 2025](https://www.startupdaily.net/topic/venture-capital/fintech-airwallex-is-blackbirds-new-canva-as-the-vc-devalues-workplace-software-unicorns-culture-amp-and-safety-culture/)
- Two layoff rounds in 2.5 years (Apr 2023: 90 roles; Nov 2025: 60 roles)
- Partnership implication: the company may be in a capital-raise or M&A process in the next 12–18 months. An acquisition by a major HRIS vendor (likely Workday, SAP, or Salesforce given Salesforce Ventures participation) could disrupt or alter a partnership agreement.

**Leadership transition (moderate)**
- Founder CEO handed off to an externally recruited CFO-turned-CEO in January 2026 — a significant transition after 15 years. Caroline Rawlinson has operational finance credibility (Trade Me, MYOB) but no stated background in HR tech product strategy. First 12–18 months will determine execution coherence.
- CMO churn (18-month tenure) creates go-to-market instability during a critical growth phase.

**Regulatory:** No ASIC, ACCC, or other regulatory actions found. Clean.

**Litigation:** No court filings or litigation press coverage found. Clean.

**Security:** UpGuard A-grade security rating (852/950) but flagged a **provisional infostealer malware finding** associated with Culture Amp systems as of May 2026. Source: [UpGuard, May 2026](https://www.upguard.com/security-report/culture-amp). This is an automated signal, not a confirmed breach. It should be raised directly with Culture Amp in technical due diligence, particularly given that their platform handles sensitive employee sentiment data.

**Integration reliability:** Multiple Capterra reviews flag HRIS data sync issues (Rippling, Google Calendar). For an HR platform integration, this is directly relevant — the surface area for sync failures is exactly where your integration would operate. Evaluate their API documentation and webhook reliability in technical diligence.
