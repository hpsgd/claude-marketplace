## Output

**Time Tracking — User Guide**

**Who can do this:** All team members can log time. Managers and Admins can view utilisation reports. Only Admins can enable or disable time tracking for the workspace.

**Before you start:** Time tracking must be enabled for your workspace (Settings → Time Tracking → Enable). If you don't see the Time Tracking option, contact your workspace admin.

**Log time against a task:**

1. Open the task you worked on.
   You'll see the task detail panel on the right.
2. Click the clock icon (⏱) in the task toolbar, or select Log time from the task menu (···).
   A "Log time" dialog opens.
3. Enter the time spent (e.g. 1h 30m or 1.5h).
   The Hours and Minutes fields accept both formats.
4. Optionally, add a note to describe what you worked on.
5. Click Log time.
   You'll see your logged time added below the task description. The task's total tracked time updates.

**Set an estimate:**

1. Open the task.
2. Click Add estimate (shown when no estimate is set) or the existing estimate value.
3. Enter the estimated time (e.g. 2h).
   The estimate appears next to the time tracking entry. If logged time exceeds the estimate, it turns red.

**View utilisation reports:**

1. Go to Reports → Utilisation (requires Manager or Admin role).
2. Filter by team, date range, or project.
3. The report shows logged time vs estimates per person and project.

**Troubleshooting:**

I can't see the time tracking option on a task:
- Time tracking may be disabled for your workspace. Ask your admin to check Settings → Time Tracking.
- You may not have permission to log time on this project. Check with your project admin.

My time isn't showing in the utilisation report:
- Reports refresh every 15 minutes. Wait and reload.
- Check you're looking at the correct date range and team filter.

I entered the wrong time — how do I fix it:
- Click the entry in the task detail view and select Edit or Delete.

**Related guides:** How to view your team's utilisation. How to set project budgets. How to enable time tracking for your workspace (Admin).
